"Get to Know My Child" Flyer Personalizer

A "Get to Know My Child" Flyer can be a great way to introduce your child to educators. It can start a dialogue between parents and educators about what the child needs to succeed in the classroom.

Follow the steps to create a flyer personalized with information about your child, a picture of your child, and ways for educators to contact you. Any information you enter will not be stored by the South Carolina Cancer Alliance.
